VIVERE: To Live Drug-Free

A community-based platform to detect, prevent, and address drug usage among youth through collaboration between schools, law enforcement, and communities.

Learn More Report Incident

How VIVERE Works

Our collaborative approach brings together key stakeholders to protect our youth

🏫

Schools

Schools upload anonymous data on drug-related incidents and trends, enabling early detection and intervention.

👮

Law Enforcement

Police departments share anonymized drug arrest data to identify patterns and high-risk areas.

🏘️

Community

Community organizations coordinate prevention programs and support services based on real data.

👥

Public Reporting

Citizens can anonymously report suspicious activities or concerns through a secure portal.
