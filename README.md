To proactively identify and prevent drug abuse by leveraging technology for early detection and promoting social awareness and community engagement.

### Project Description
Vivere combines technological tools with social initiatives to educate, engage, and empower communities, ensuring children and young adults are supported in environments free from drug abuse.

### The Problem statement
The rising cases of drug abuse among teenagers and young adults pose a serious societal threat.
